Australian shepherds have a double coat of fur which effectively protects them against all types of weather. A tougher outer coat acts as a waterproof barrier while their fluffier undercoat acts as insulation in hot and cold temperatures. This makes them hearty companions on farms and ranches, outdoor sports, or for those who live in regions that have climate variations.
